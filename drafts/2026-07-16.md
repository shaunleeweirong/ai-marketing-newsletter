## AI Use Cases of the Week

### ⭐ [Reddit deploys AI to catch 25K daily GEO spam posts gaming ChatGPT and Gemini](https://thenextweb.com/news/reddit-ai-marketing-slop-geo-crackdown)
Brands are seeding fake Reddit posts to surface in AI chatbot answers — a practice called Generative Engine Optimization (GEO). Agency ReachLLM confirmed its posts appear in ChatGPT responses within a day. Reddit now catches 25,000 spammy posts daily using its own LLM and has reduced spam exposure by 20%. Cornell Tech researchers found planted text as short as 13 words can steer AI agents toward fabricated products with up to 100% success. **Why it matters:** GEO is simultaneously a real marketing channel and a brand-safety minefield — practitioners need to understand both the offense and defense sides of this rapidly evolving tactic.
_Source: The Next Web / Bloomberg · Engagement: covered by Bloomberg, Reuters, MediaPost, 10+ outlets · Relevance: Direct_
