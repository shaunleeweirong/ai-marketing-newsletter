# AI for Marketers — 2026-07-11

Strong week: Google finally lets you measure AI search visibility, ChatGPT Ads gets serious targeting, and TikTok's creative AI takes a leap.

## The Signal

### [Google Search Console Now Reports Your Visibility in AI Overviews](https://developers.google.com/search/blog/2026/06/gen-ai-performance-reports)
Google is rolling out its Generative AI Performance Report to more sites as of this week. You can now see how often your URLs appear as citations in AI Overviews and AI Mode — broken down by page, country, device, and date. No click data yet, but this is the first official KPI for AI search visibility. If you're investing in GEO, you finally have a dashboard to prove it's working.

_Source: Google Search Central Blog_

### [ChatGPT Ads Quietly Adds Custom Audience Targeting](https://www.jonloomer.com/chatgpt-ads-custom-audiences/)
OpenAI added custom audience targeting to ChatGPT Ads with no formal announcement — advertisers discovered it themselves. You can now upload hashed email/phone lists to target or exclude users (minimum 100K identifiers). Combined with new AI ad-copy generation and bid adjustments, ChatGPT Ads is building Meta/Google-level infrastructure faster than most teams have noticed.

_Source: Jon Loomer Digital_

### [Google's June 2026 Spam Update Rolled Out in Two Days — and It's Substantial](https://searchengineland.com/google-releases-june-2026-spam-update-481002)
The June spam update completed in just 48 hours and is hitting hard. Primary targets: sites publishing hundreds of pages with minimal original value — i.e., AI content farms. Google is evaluating overall site quality rather than individual pages, so a few bad articles can drag down your entire domain. If you're using AI to produce content at scale without editorial oversight, this is your warning shot.

_Source: Search Engine Land_

### [TikTok Symphony Gets Next-Gen Video AI and an Agentic Hub Marketplace](https://newsroom.tiktok.com/tiktok-world-26-turning-discovery-into-business-growth-with-ai-powered-innovations-vertical-experiences-and-high-impact-brand-solutions?lang=en)
TikTok's Symphony creative platform now integrates ByteDance's Seedance 2.0 video model for higher-quality AI-generated ad creative, plus a "Reference to Video" feature giving frame-by-frame control. The bigger news: Agentic Hub, a new marketplace of first- and third-party AI skills for campaign creation, performance analysis, and catalog management. Smart+ Auto Selection automatically picks winning creative across human, product, and AI-generated variants.

_Source: TikTok Newsroom_

## Tool of the Week
