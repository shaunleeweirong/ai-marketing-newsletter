## AI Use Cases of the Week

### ⭐ [41% of LinkedIn long-form posts are now fully AI-generated — Pangram Labs study](https://www.pangram.com/blog/ai-in-your-feed)
Pangram Labs analyzed 1M+ posts across LinkedIn, Medium, X, Substack, and Reddit. LinkedIn topped the list at 40.5% fully AI-generated long-form content (250+ words), nearly double X at 24%. Reddit and Substack hover around 10%. The study lands weeks before the EU AI Act Article 50 disclosure requirements take effect on August 2. **Why it matters:** If you're writing LinkedIn content, you're competing with a flood of AI slop — and LinkedIn's own "Enhance Post" button contributes to the problem. Authenticity and original data are now competitive advantages, not nice-to-haves. The upcoming EU disclosure rules will reshape this landscape fast.
